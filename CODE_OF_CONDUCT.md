# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to kaeltheforgekeeper@outlook.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
