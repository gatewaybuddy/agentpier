## Package Overview

This document provides a comprehensive comparison of AgentPier Trust Score (APTS) packages across industry verticals, helping enterprises select the appropriate compliance framework for their AI agent deployments.

**Available Packages:**
- **Base APTS:** Core trust framework for general AI agents
- **Healthcare Package:** HIPAA, FDA, and clinical safety extensions (+$299/mo)
- **Financial Services Package:** SOX, PCI DSS, and banking compliance extensions (+$399/mo)
- **Government Package:** FedRAMP, NIST, and federal compliance extensions (+$499/mo)

### Selection Criteria Matrix

| Selection Criteria | Weight | Base APTS | Healthcare | Financial | Government |
|-------------------|--------|-----------|------------|-----------|------------|
| **Regulatory Requirements** | 40% | General | HIPAA/FDA | SOX/PCI/SEC | FedRAMP/NIST |
| **Data Sensitivity** | 25% | Standard | PHI | PCI/Financial | CUI/Classified |
| **Audit Frequency** | 20% | Annual | Quarterly | Quarterly | Continuous |
| **Risk Tolerance** | 10% | Moderate | Low | Low | Very Low |
| **Budget Constraints** | 5% | Low cost | Medium cost | Higher cost | Highest cost |

### Decision Tree

```
Is your AI agent handling regulated data?
├─ No → Base APTS
└─ Yes → What type of data?
    ├─ Healthcare/PHI → Healthcare Package
    ├─ Financial/PCI → Financial Package
    ├─ Government/CUI → Government Package
    └─ Multiple types → Multi-industry custom
```

### Risk-Based Tier Selection

**Professional Tier Suitable For:**
- Non-critical business applications
- Limited regulatory exposure
- Internal-facing systems only
- Budget-conscious deployments

**Enterprise Tier Required For:**
- Mission-critical applications
- High regulatory exposure
- External-facing/customer systems
- Zero-tolerance for compliance failures
